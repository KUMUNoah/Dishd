Privacy Policy

Effective July 22, 2026 · Last updated July 22, 2026

This policy explains what data dishd collects, why, and what control you have over it. The short version: we collect only what the app needs to work, we don't sell your data, and you can delete everything yourself, in the app, at any time.

What we collect

  • Account info — your name, username, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash; we never see it in plain text).
  • Content you create — recipe links you save, reviews, ratings, photos you post, your profile photo, follows, likes, and blocks.
  • Cooking goals — if you set them, how often you want to cook and how many new recipes you want to try in a year. Goals are private to you; other users never see them or your progress.
  • Usage data — in-app events (like "saved a recipe" or "posted a review") tied to your account, used to understand how dishd is used and improve it.

We do not collect your contacts, precise location, phone number, or any payment information. We don't track you across other apps or websites, and we don't show ads.

How we use it

  • To run the app: show your reviews to your followers, build your feed, and sync your saved recipes.
  • To keep dishd safe: act on reports, enforce blocks, and moderate content.
  • To improve the product, using aggregated usage data.

Who can see your content

Your reviews and profile are visible to other dishd users. If your account is private, your reviews are visible only to followers you've approved — everywhere in the app. Blocking a user hides your content from them and theirs from you, completely.

Where your data lives

Data is stored with Supabase, our backend provider, on servers in the United States. We share data with no one else — no data brokers, no advertisers.

Deleting your data

Settings → Delete account permanently erases your profile, reviews, photos, saves, follows, and all other account data. This happens immediately and can't be undone. You can also email us to request deletion.

How long we keep it

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, your profile, reviews, photos, saves, follows, and goals are erased immediately and permanently. Reports you've filed are kept without your identity attached, so we can act on repeat abuse. Aggregated, non-identifying usage counts may remain in our analytics.

Security

Access to your data is enforced at the database level: you must be signed in to read anything, and a private account's content is served only to followers you've approved. Passwords are hashed by our auth provider and never visible to us. No system is perfectly secure, but we design for the principle that the app should only ever be able to fetch what you're allowed to see.

Children

dishd is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child under 13 has an account, contact us and we'll delete it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including California and the EU), you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Deletion and correction are available directly in the app; for anything else, email us.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll notify you in the app before they take effect.
